About The Excellent Life Conference

The TEL conference is more than an event - it is a divine appointment for transformation inspired by Genesis 23 and Psalm 1:3. We believe that to truly thrive is to be deeply rooted in God, to advance in obedience despite opposition, and to progress in a way that blesses others. Thrive encapsulates growth, advance, and progress (GAP) not just as goals but as outcomes of an encounter with God. Over these three days, the conference is designed to shape who we become, not just what we receive.

Beyond personal edification, this conference is a bridge for others to experience transformation through those who attended. It is a believers' meeting, but the atmosphere is open to all who are ready for transformation.
